Mickey Mouse

A remarkable life

This is the story of a remarkable character who overcame many hurdles in life. Although he never went without food, water or shelter. There were troubling times for this young chap. Life wasn't always a bed of roses and he sometimes knew that. At an early age? Perhaps not, but with a busy home life, lots of comings and goings with family and family friends, he never really knew his place. Or did he? More often than not, he looked to others for guidance and perharps validation. For what? Not he even knew. School was bit of an escape, where he could become something. This was often drummed into him and he took note of that. Often the careful one, he wanted to please everyone and anyone to maybe earn something. High school was very much the same and he struggled with fitting in with any group of friends. He was more comfortable in small groups, even one to one and on his own. Then university followed the same path. With many ups and downs along the way reachin university was a pretty big milestone, but the same validation mechanism followed him. Hi escape now seems to be writing code whilst listening to Ludovico Einaudi and Riopy.

In this section we will talk more about Mickey's life as he entered adulthood and how his life changed after university and entering the big bad world of work. He unfortunately didn't go into the world of web development which is obvsiously a shame!
